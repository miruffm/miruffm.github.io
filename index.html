MIRU | Mycobacterial Infectious Research Unit | Infectious Diseases | Mycobacteriology | Computational Microbiology | Data science

We are based at the University Hospital Frankfurt. Our research group uses a variety of epidemiological, clinical, microbiological and bioinformatical methods and conducts research in several fields of infectious diseases, including tuberculosis and non-tuberculous mycobacterial infection.

On this homepage, we share information regarding ongoing projects as well as the latest news. If you are willing to contribute to one of our studies or search collaboration partners in the field of mycobacterial infection research, you are most welcome to contact us.

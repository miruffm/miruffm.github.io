New publication in Scientific Reports

Jun 10, 2024

In collaboration with the Department of Radiology at University Hospital Frankfurt, we investigated the radiological features of patients infected or colonised with slow-growing non-tuberculous mycobacteria. We applied a score, originally described for MAC pulmonary disease, to other SGM-species. We are very happy that the manuscript has been accepted for publication in Scientific Reports, now.

You can find the paper here, at miru-ffm.org, the journal's homepage, or at PubMed.
